# My Hyprland Dotfiles

These are **my personal configuration files** for Hyprland on Arch Linux. They are tailored to my specific setup, preferences, and workflow — feel free to look around, get inspired, or borrow ideas, but **don’t expect a plug-and-play experience**.

> ⚠️ **Warning:** I don't recommend copying this Hyprland config as-is.  
> You’ll almost certainly need to tweak or rewrite parts of it to match your own system, monitor layout, GPU quirks, and overall preferences.

## Why I’m Sharing This

I’m sharing these configs mostly as a reference — not a polished framework.  
I believe it’s better to write your own Hyprland setup from scratch, or at least understand what every line does.

If you're just starting out, I recommend:

- Or even better: **build your own config** with help from an AI assistant or the [Hyprland Wiki](https://wiki.hyprland.org/)

## What’s Inside

This repo contains:

- `~/.config/hypr/` – Hyprland config
- Some scripts, themes, and helper configs
- Stuff I use daily, and occasionally refactor

It’s probably **incomplete**, **messy**, and reflects my current mood/ideas — which is also why it's fun.
